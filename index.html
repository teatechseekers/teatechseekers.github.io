About Us

TeaTechSeekers Webpage is an academic platform created by Myanmar M.Sc. students studying at Assam Agricultural University. Our mission is to promote tea science through research, innovation, academic collaboration, and knowledge sharing for students, researchers, and tea professionals around the world.
